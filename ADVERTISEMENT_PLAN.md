# LatentDB 30-Second AI Video Advertisement Plan

## Goal

Create a polished 30-second AI-generated video that positions LatentDB as a governed dynamic enterprise data plane: flexible like a no-code builder, but controlled by kernel-enforced security, workflow, audit, and AI visibility.

## Core Message

“Build tenant-specific business systems without losing governance.”

## Target Audience

- Startup founders building internal tools or vertical SaaS.
- CTOs and platform teams replacing brittle CRM/ERP customizations.
- Enterprise operators who need dynamic workflows, permissions, audit, and AI-safe data access.

## Tone

Confident, modern, technical, and enterprise-grade. Avoid hype-heavy AI language. The product should feel like serious infrastructure, not a toy builder.

## Visual Style

- Clean SaaS/product interface shots.
- Dark-on-light operational dashboards with precise motion.
- Subtle animated schema lines, permission gates, workflow transitions, and audit trails.
- No cartoon mascots, no generic robot imagery, no excessive neon.
- UI should look like a real production console.

## 30-Second Script

### 0-4s — Problem

Visual: Fragmented spreadsheets, rigid CRM fields, approval chains, and scattered internal tools.

Voiceover:
“Every business has its own data model. Most software forces it into someone else’s.”

On-screen text:
`Rigid apps slow teams down.`

### 4-10s — Product Reveal

Visual: LatentDB Operations Console opens. Builder page creates an Invoice object with fields, permissions, workflow, and AI visibility.

Voiceover:
“LatentDB lets each tenant build governed business objects without code.”

On-screen text:
`Dynamic objects. Kernel-enforced rules.`

### 10-17s — Governance

Visual: Permission checks, restricted fields, workflow approval, audit event, and AI visibility toggles animate around the object definition.

Voiceover:
“Schema validation, RBAC, field restrictions, workflow, approvals, and audit are enforced at the platform layer.”

On-screen text:
`Dynamic never means unsafe.`

### 17-23s — AI Safety

Visual: AI agent answers a question with citations while sensitive fields remain locked.

Voiceover:
“AI can search business records, but only the fields it is allowed to see.”

On-screen text:
`AI visibility. Citations. No bypass.`

### 23-28s — Outcome

Visual: Records, Reports, Builder, Schema, and Action Planner all showing the newly published object.

Voiceover:
“Publish once. Use it everywhere: records, reports, workflows, agents, and action planning.”

On-screen text:
`One governed data plane.`

### 28-30s — Close

Visual: LatentDB logo and product console.

Voiceover:
“LatentDB. Build the business model. Keep the rules.”

On-screen text:
`LatentDB`

## Shot List

1. Rigid systems montage: spreadsheet columns, disconnected tools, approval emails.
2. Product hero: LatentDB Operations Console.
3. Builder flow: create object, add fields, set workflow, configure AI visibility.
4. Governance animation: tenant boundary, RBAC gate, restricted field lock, audit row.
5. AI answer with citations and hidden sensitive data.
6. Published object appearing in Records, Schema, Reports, and Action Planner.
7. Logo end card.

## Prompt For AI Video Generation

Create a 30-second premium SaaS advertisement for “LatentDB,” a governed dynamic enterprise data platform. Show a realistic production-grade operations console where a tenant admin creates a custom Invoice object, adds fields, configures permissions, workflow approvals, audit, and AI visibility. Use clean enterprise UI visuals, subtle motion graphics, and precise interface animations. Avoid cartoon robots, mascots, neon sci-fi imagery, or generic stock business scenes. The message is: dynamic business objects with kernel-enforced governance. Include scenes of records, reports, schema, AI answers with citations, restricted fields locked, and approval-gated action planning. End with the text: “LatentDB — Build the business model. Keep the rules.”

- `Schema. RBAC. Workflow. Audit.`

## Music And Sound

- Minimal, confident electronic score.
- Light interface clicks and transition sounds.
- No dramatic trailer effects.
- Keep voiceover clear and slightly understated.

## Deliverables

- 30-second primary video, 16:9.
- 30-second vertical crop, 9:16.
- 15-second cutdown for social.
- Static end card with logo and tagline.
- Captioned version for silent autoplay.

## Success Criteria

- Viewer understands LatentDB is dynamic but governed.
- Product feels production-ready and enterprise-safe.
- AI is presented as controlled and permission-aware, not magical.
- The Builder feature is the hero, but kernel enforcement is the differentiator.
